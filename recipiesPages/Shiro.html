Shiro

Description

Shiro is a staple vegetarian dish in Ethiopian cuisine. It is a stew or curry made from ground dried chickpeas and various spices. Just like any other Ethiopian dish, it is served with injera.

Ingredients

  • Shiro Powder
  • Onion
  • Garlic(minced)
  • berbere spice
  • Niter Kibbeh
  • water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat the niter qibeh over medium heat
  2. Add finely chopped onion and saute it until it turns golden brown
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute
  4. Add shiro Powder
  5. Add the berbere spice to the pot and mix well.
  6. Add water and simmer
  7. Season and serve!
