Lasagna Recipie

Description

A classic homemade lasagna recipe. Perfect for weeknight dinners, or to take to friends for a special occation. This recipe can be frozen so it is perfect to be made ahead of time.

Ingredients List

  • Onion
  • Olive Oil
  • beef
  • Mariana sause
  • Ricotta chesse
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps:

  1. Start by making a homemade meat sauce
  2. Make a parmeasan ricotta cheese mixture
  3. Use your favorite lasagna noodles to make your layers
  4. Bake covered for 15 minutes
